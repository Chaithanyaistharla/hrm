How to use bulk upload

  1. Download the CSV template below
  2. Fill in the payslip data for each employee
  3. Upload the completed CSV file
  4. Review and confirm the payslips before saving

CSV Template

Download the template file to get started with the correct format

Required CSV Columns:

employee_email - Employee's email address
month - Month (1-12)
year - Year (e.g., 2024)
basic - Basic salary amount
hra - House Rent Allowance (optional)
allowances - Other allowances (optional)
overtime_hours - Overtime hours (optional)
overtime_pay - Overtime pay amount (optional)
bonus - Bonus amount (optional)
income_tax - Income tax deduction (optional)
provident_fund - PF deduction (optional)
professional_tax - Professional tax (optional)
deductions - Other deductions (optional)
status - DRAFT, PROCESSED, PAID, or CANCELLED
pay_date - Payment date (YYYY-MM-DD, optional)
notes - Additional notes (optional)
